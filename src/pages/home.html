Communitas Hospitii

Welcome! We live by the ancient philosophy of Hospitium, the divine right of guests and the divine duty of the host.

We will be operating a hostel, a bed and breakfast, and a number of Airbnbs.

We are a 501(d) organization in connection with Communio Templorum Romanorum, a religious organization.

Sepulcretum

We are a 501(c)(13) non-profit cemetery.

Hospitium Land Trust

We are a 501(c)(2) land trust. We own the lands for various Airbnbs around the world.

Profits are distributed to Communitas Hospitii, Sepulcretum, and Communio Templorum Romanorum.
